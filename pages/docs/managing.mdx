When managing a large group of devices, organization can become hard. This section focuses on the ways to organize your devices and see information on them at a glance.

## Features

- With [labels](/docs/managing/labels), you can organize your devices
- With [filters](/docs/managing/filters), you can query your devices
- And with [device status](/docs/managing/status), you can see which devices are online
